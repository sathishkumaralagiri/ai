## 🎯 What is this?

This repository is a practitioner's field guide to **Generative AI in agile environments**. It covers how to choose the right AI tools, how to write effective prompts, how to integrate AI into your ceremonies, and how to coach teams through the change.

Everything here is based on real experience — not theory.

## 🧠 Prompt engineering fundamentals

Every effective AI prompt for agile work follows this structure:

```
ROLE     → "You are an experienced Scrum Master..."
CONTEXT  → Paste your team's actual data, notes, or inputs
TASK     → One clear, specific instruction
FORMAT   → "Produce a table / 3 bullet points / 150-word summary..."
TONE     → "Write for a senior leadership audience / for the team"
```

The single biggest mistake practitioners make is giving the AI too little context. More context = better output.

## ⚖️ Using AI responsibly

AI is a thinking partner, not a decision-maker. Keep these principles in mind:

- **Never paste personally identifiable information** into a consumer AI tool
- **Always review and edit** AI output before sharing — your name is on it
- **Be transparent with your team** when AI has helped produce an artefact
- **AI amplifies your coaching** — it doesn't replace the human relationship

See [`ethics/responsible-ai.md`](./ethics/responsible-ai.md) for the full guide.
