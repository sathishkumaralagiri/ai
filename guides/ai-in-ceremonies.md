# AI in agile ceremonies

> Where AI genuinely helps, where it doesn't, and how to integrate it without replacing the human work.

---

## The guiding principle

AI is best used **before and after** ceremonies — for preparation and documentation. It is not a replacement for the facilitation, conversation, and coaching that happens *in the room*.

---

## Sprint Planning

### Before (high value)
- Draft Sprint Goal options from planned stories
- Calculate adjusted capacity factoring in absences
- Prepare refinement questions for top stories
- Spot risks in the proposed plan

### During (low value)
- AI can't read the room, sense team energy, or navigate conflict
- Don't use AI during live planning — focus on the team

### After (high value)
- Write the post-planning stakeholder summary
- Document the Sprint Goal and commitments
- Draft the planning email to wider stakeholders

---

## Daily Scrum

### Before (moderate value)
- Analyse patterns across recent daily scrums if you keep notes
- Prepare coaching questions if you notice a trend

### During (not recommended)
- The Daily Scrum is 15 minutes — AI adds friction, not value here

### After (moderate value)
- Log patterns and impediments for retrospective data

---

## Sprint Review

### Before (high value)
- Write the increment narrative in business language
- Prepare demo talking points for each story
- Draft the stakeholder update

### During (low value)
- Focus on the conversation and feedback — capture notes manually or with a transcription tool

### After (high value)
- Synthesise stakeholder feedback into themes
- Produce the full review summary
- Update the product backlog based on decisions made

---

## Retrospective

### Before (high value)
- Generate facilitation plan for your chosen format
- Review last sprint's action items and prepare an update

### During (low value — with one exception)
- Don't use AI to synthesise live — it slows the session
- **Exception:** if you use a digital board (Miro/Mural), you can paste inputs into AI at the end of the diverge phase to speed up clustering

### After (very high value)
- Synthesise all inputs into themes
- Run 5 Whys on the top theme
- Draft SMART action items
- Write the retro summary for the wiki

---

## PI Planning (SAFe)

### Before (very high value)
- Prepare the pre-PI pack and team briefing
- Draft initial PI Objectives from features
- Calculate PI capacity

### During (moderate value)
- Synthesise dependency risks in real time between breakouts
- Draft ROAM board entries as risks are raised

### After (very high value)
- Produce the PI confidence vote summary
- Document dependencies and risk register
- Write the PI Planning readout for leadership
